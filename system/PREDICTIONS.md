# PREDICTIONS.md — Decision Calibration Log

Before significant decisions, write a prediction. After the outcome, fill in the rest.
Over time, patterns emerge in where your model is miscalibrated.

---

## Format

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — [decision/event]
**Prediction:** [what I expect to happen]
**Confidence:** High / Medium / Low
**Outcome:** [fill in after]
**Delta:** [what surprised me]
**Lesson:** [what to update in my model]
```
