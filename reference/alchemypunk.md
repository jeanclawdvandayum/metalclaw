# Alchemypunk — Style Guide

## What Is It
Cultural/aesthetic movement for Alchemix. Third path between cyberpunk (corporate dystopia warning) and solarpunk (utopian garden dreams). Alchemypunk = transmute the chains into tools, hand the tools to everyone.

## Core Framing
- Financial system = Archons/Demiurge (Gnostic oppressors)
- V3 = The Gnosis (liberating knowledge)
- Self-repaying loans = democratized fractional reserve banking
- Alchemix brand woven in at strategic revelation moments, not plastered everywhere

## Palette — Copper on Obsidian
| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Obsidian | `#050505` | Backgrounds |
| Deep obsidian | `#1a110c` | Panels, depth |
| Burnt copper | `#6b3a20` | Borders, accents |
| Warm copper | `#c8854e` | Primary text, highlights |
| Alchemist gold | `#f2dab8` | Headlines, emphasis |

## Visual Language
- **CRT aesthetic:** Scanlines, vignette, grain noise, boot sequences
- **Glitch effects:** Chromatic aberration, displacement jitter, flicker, VHS tracking
- **Alchemical symbols:** ☿ ⚗ ☉ △ ∞ ◯ ⊕, Ouroboros
- **Hidden text:** Nag Hammadi fragments at ~2.5% opacity (easter eggs for the curious)
- **Typography:** Monospace for code/data, serif for narrative
- **No sound effects** — frame as spiritual awakening, not a game

## Canon Works
1. **The Great Work** — Manifesto (9 Gnostic chapters)
2. **The Grimoire** — V3 mechanics as alchemical rituals
3. **The Mirror** — Extraction calculator (shows hidden cost of traditional finance)
4. **The Signal** — Memes, social templates, identity kit
5. **The Seal** — FlameBearer NFT (soulbound, on-chain SVG)

## Tone
- Reverent but not preachy
- Technical accuracy is non-negotiable
- Mysterious, not confusing
- Empowering, not elitist
- "We found fire. Now we're sharing it."

## Anti-Patterns
- ❌ Corporate marketing language ("revolutionary platform", "next-gen DeFi")
- ❌ Alchemix logo plastered everywhere (reveal at strategic moments)
- ❌ Generic crypto aesthetic (blue gradients, blockchain graphics)
- ❌ Inaccurate V3 mechanics (always verify against v3-mechanics.md)
